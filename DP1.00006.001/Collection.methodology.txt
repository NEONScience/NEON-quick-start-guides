Secondary - tipping bucket located on the top of the tower infrastructure. Throughfall - tipping buckets at 4 or 5 soil array locations (absent at short-stature ecosystems). On flat ground the throughfall collection area is between 37.5 and 50 cm aboveground. Bulk precipitation is determined at one- and thirty-minute intervals for secondary and throughfall precipitation.

In all data Releases prior to RELEASE-2025, Primary precipitation (weighing gauge) was also included in DP1.00006.001. Weighing gauge precipitation can now be found in DP1.00044.001.
