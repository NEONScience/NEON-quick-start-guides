The dataQF and dataQFSciRvw fields in each record are quality flags for known issues applying to the record, added by NEON Science upon data review. In the wdp_chemLab table there are flags indicating any measurement/detection issues encountered by the external lab. Field-level data quality information with accompanying remarks can be found in the wdp_collection and wdp_collectionChem tables, in the equipmentProblems and chemSubsampleCondition fields.
