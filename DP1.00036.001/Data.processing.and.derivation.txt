Data is processed using the eddy4R codebase (Metzger et al. 2017; Xu et al., 2019). For a detailed description of data product derivation, please see the Eddy-Covariance Data Product Bundle ATBD (NEON.DOC.004571).

Atmospheric CO2 isotopes data product is bundled into DP4.00200, not available as a standalone download, and is published in 9- and 30-minute averaging intervals for both sampling periods and validation periods.
