Data is processed using the eddy4R codebase (Metzger et al. 2017; Xu et al., 2019). For a detailed description of data product derivation, please see the Eddy-Covariance Data Product Bundle ATBD (NEON.DOC.004571).

Atmospheric H2O isotopes data product is bundled into DP4.00200, not available as a standalone download, and is published in 9- and 30- minutes averaging intervals for sampling periods and 3- and 30- minutes for validation periods.
