RGB and RGB+NIR images are captured using a Stardot NetCam on the bottom of all terrestrial towers to study below-canopy phenology. The camera also captures images of snowdepth stakes.  Every 15 minutes each camera captures back-to-back RGB and RGB+NIR images separated by 30 seconds. Every second image (i.e., 30 minutes) is directly transmitted to the Phenocam Network for analysis, archiving, and display.
