Precipitation - weighing gauge is housed within a small double fence intercomparison reference, which is generally located within 0.5 km of the tower infrastructure. Bulk precipitation is determined at one-hour and one-day intervals for precipitation- weighing gauge. The Documentation section of this guide includes a list of precipitation collector types and quantities at each NEON site, along with the nearest terrestrial site for accessing precipitation data for aquatic sites without collectors (NEON Precipitation Sensors by Site).
