Results of pathogen tests on small mammal blood and ear samples from 2020 onward. Small mammal ear tissue is tested for Borrelia burgdorferi, while small mammal blood samples are tested for a variety of other tick-borne pathogens, including: Borrellia spp., Anaplasma phagocytophilum, Rickettsia ricketsii, and Francisella tularensis.
