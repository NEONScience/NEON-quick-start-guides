The mcc_soilSequenceVariantMetadata_16S_in and mcc_soilSequenceVariantMetadata_ITS_in can be joined by the dnaSampleID. The mcc_soilTaxonTableMetadata_16S and mcc_soilTaxonTableMetadata_ITS tables can be joined by the dnaSampleID. Any of the mcc metadata tables (16S or ITS) can be joined to the mcc_labSummary table using the field testMethod. Any of the mcc metadata tables can be joined with the soil field data using the geneticSampleID.
