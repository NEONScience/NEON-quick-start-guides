All tables in this data product can be joined by the dnaSampleID. Tables can be joined among other soil collection data by linking the genomicsSampleID field of this data to the geneticSampleID or genomicsPooledIDList in table sls_metagenomicsPooling of the Soil Physical Properties data product, and from there using the sampleID to link to other soil analyses. Soil microbe community composition, Soil microbe group abundance data, and Soil microbe marker gene sequences can be linked with this product using the dnaSampleID field.
