Measured using YSI EXO2 Multisondes located at both the S1 (upstream) and S2 (downstream) locations of wadeable streams and on the buoy at lakes and river sites. At wadeable stream sites measurements are collected every minute. At lakes and river sites measurements are collected at 0.5 m below the surface every 5 minutes, and a vertical profile of the water coulmn collected every 4 hours.
