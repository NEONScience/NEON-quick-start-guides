This data product contains one GeoTIFF raster, the Canopy Height Model, which has 1 m pixels whose value is the height of the top of the vegetation canopy above the ground immediately below it. Missing pixels are assigned a value of -9999. All AOP L3 data, including CHM, are distributed in 1 km by 1 km tiles which include the Easting and Northing coordinates (in that order) of their southwest corner in their file names.
