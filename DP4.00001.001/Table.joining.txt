Summary weather statistics are be generated from Level 1 (L1) data products at NEON core sites. The L1 data products used to generate summary weather statistics are 2D wind speed (DP1.00001.001), Triple aspirated air temperature (DP1.00003.001), Barometric pressure (DP1.00004.001), Primary precipitation (DP1.00006.001), Shortwave radiation (primary pyranometer) (DP1.00022.001), and Relative humidity (DP1.00098.001).
