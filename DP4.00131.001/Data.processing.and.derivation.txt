There are known issues when using Google Earth to view .KMZ Maps. If you are experienceing problems viewing these morphology layers it may be helpful to turn off habitat unit polygons in order to view the geomorphic feature polygons. For a more in-depth look at the processing please see the NEON Standard Operating Procedure: Post-Processing of Wadeable Stream Morphology Data from NEON Wadeable Streams (NEON.DOC.004857)
