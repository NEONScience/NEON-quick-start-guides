NSAE is measured using the eddy-covariance method, sampling data at each measurement level of the tower and in the soil plots. There are 20+ data products from level 1 (dp01) to level 4 (dp04) included in this bundle, each measured using different methods, at different sampling rates, and producing data at different intervals. For more detailed information, please see the individual data product guides. The meteorological and soil data products in this bundled file are also available as stand-alone data product files.
